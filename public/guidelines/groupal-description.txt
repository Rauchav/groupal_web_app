  WHAT IS GROUPAL?
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

Groupal is a next-generation group-buying marketplace where buyers come
together to unlock massive discounts on high-value products. The platform is
built on one powerful, simple idea:

  THE MORE BUYERS THAT JOIN, THE BIGGER THE DISCOUNT FOR EVERYONE.

Unlike traditional e-commerce where every buyer pays the same price regardless
of how many people are shopping, Groupal operates on a linear progressive
discount model. Every single new buyer who joins a group buy pushes the price
down a little more — for every existing participant. The group wins together.

Groupal connects verified sellers with motivated buyer communities, creating a
win-win dynamic that transforms online shopping from a solo transaction into a
collective, social, and far more rewarding experience.

  THE CORE MECHANIC — LINEAR PROGRESSIVE DISCOUNT
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

Each group buy deal has:

  · An ORIGINAL PRICE   — the full retail / in-store price
  · A MAXIMUM DISCOUNT  — the best discount possible (e.g. 50%)
  · A BUYER CAP         — the maximum number of buyers needed to unlock it
  · A DEADLINE          — the date/time when the deal closes

The discount formula is linear:

  Discount per buyer = Max Discount % ÷ Max Buyers Required
  Current Discount   = Current Buyer Count × Discount Per Buyer

Example:
  Product: 65" Smart TV — Original Price: $1,799
  Max Discount: 50% — Max Buyers: 40

  · With 1 buyer   → 1.25% off  → $1,776.52
  · With 10 buyers → 12.5% off  → $1,574.13
  · With 20 buyers → 25% off    → $1,349.25
  · With 30 buyers → 37.5% off  → $1,124.37
  · With 40 buyers → 50% off    → $899.50

Every buyer benefits from every new person who joins — including you.
This creates genuine social incentive to share and recruit.

  HOW IT WORKS — STEP BY STEP (BUYER JOURNEY)
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

  STEP 1 — BROWSE & PICK
  ──────────────────────
  Buyers explore active group buy deals across multiple categories: smart TVs,
  vehicles, technology, clothing, accessories, home appliances, travel
  packages, and much more. Each deal card clearly shows:

    · The product and seller (verified)
    · The current price (based on how many buyers have already joined)
    · The maximum possible discount
    · The current buyer count vs. the buyer cap
    · The countdown timer to the deal deadline
    · The savings amount already accumulated by the group

  Buyers can filter by category, sort by discount, and browse both active
  deals and successfully completed past deals to build confidence.

  ─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

  STEP 2 — RESERVE YOUR SPOT (10% UPFRONT)
  ─────────────────────────────────────────
  To join a group buy, buyers pay just 10% of the current item price as a
  reservation fee. This small upfront payment:

    · Locks in their spot in the group buy
    · Confirms their intent to purchase
    · Keeps deals serious and prevents fake demand

  Important: By paying the reservation fee, the buyer is committing to
  purchase the item. The deal is ON. The final price (and therefore the
  final 90% payment) is not yet known — it depends on how many more buyers
  join before the deal closes. The buyer's discount grows with every new
  person who joins after them.

  The reservation fee is proportional to the current price at join time,
  not the final price. This ensures early joiners always benefit more.

  ─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

  STEP 3 — SHARE & RECRUIT
  ────────────────────────
  Once inside a group buy, every participant has a powerful personal
  incentive to recruit more buyers:

    · Every new buyer they bring in reduces the price for the whole group
    · INCLUDING for themselves — their own final price drops too
    · Buyers can share deals directly via WhatsApp, X (Twitter), or a
      direct link
    · The more buyers join, the closer the group gets to maximum discount

  This creates a viral, self-reinforcing growth loop. Buyers become
  ambassadors for the deals they care about, not because they're paid to,
  but because it literally saves them more money.

  ─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

  STEP 4 — DEAL CLOSES, EVERYONE WINS
  ────────────────────────────────────
  A group buy deal closes when either:

    (a) The countdown deadline expires, OR
    (b) The maximum number of buyers is reached

  Whichever happens first triggers the deal closure. At that moment:

    · The final discount percentage is locked in and calculated precisely
    · All participants are charged the remaining 90% of the final price,
      minus their individual Groupal discount
    · The seller is notified and begins fulfillment
    · Buyers receive their items at the group-discounted price

  Example closing scenario:
  Smart TV deal, max 40 buyers, 30 joined before deadline →
  37.5% discount applied → final price $1,124.37 for all 30 buyers.

  HOW GROUPAL HELPS SELLERS — AN INCREDIBLE REVENUE OPPORTUNITY
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

  · SELL VOLUME WITHOUT ADVERTISING SPEND
    Traditional e-commerce forces sellers to spend heavily on ads to
    acquire buyers. On Groupal, the buyers recruit each other. The seller
    lists a deal once and watches the buyer count grow organically —
    driven by participants who are financially motivated to share.

  · GUARANTEED MINIMUM REVENUE
    Because every buyer commits with a 10% reservation fee, sellers know
    demand is real before they commit to fulfillment. No ghost carts,
    no abandoned checkouts — only committed purchasers.

  · PREDICTABLE BULK ORDERS
    Group buys create predictable order volumes that sellers can use to
    negotiate better supplier pricing, optimize manufacturing runs, or
    manage inventory more efficiently — creating a compounding margin
    advantage.

  · BRAND EXPOSURE TO QUALIFIED AUDIENCES
    When a group buy deal is shared across WhatsApp groups, family chats,
    and social networks, the seller's product reaches highly qualified
    audiences: people who already trust the person sharing the deal.
    This is the most valuable kind of marketing — personal recommendation.

  · FLEXIBLE DEAL STRUCTURING
    Sellers define the deal parameters: original price, maximum discount,
    buyer cap, and deadline. They set an offer they're comfortable with
    at scale. The Groupal platform does the rest.

  · ZERO INVENTORY RISK ON SPECULATIVE DEALS
    Sellers can run deals knowing they won't ship a single unit until
    the deal closes. If the group doesn't reach a viable size before
    the deadline, the seller has no obligation to fulfill.

  · DIRECT ACCESS TO MOTIVATED BUYERS
    Groupal's buyer base consists of people actively looking for the
    best deal on big-ticket items. These are high-intent shoppers,
    not casual browsers. Conversion rates on committed group buys
    are inherently higher than standard e-commerce.

  HOW GROUPAL WILL CHANGE ONLINE SHOPPING — FOR EVERYONE
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

  Today's online shopping is fundamentally individualistic. Each buyer
  navigates a sea of products alone, pays a fixed price, and moves on.
  The relationship ends at checkout. Sellers compete on tiny margins,
  pour billions into advertising, and pray for repeat customers.

  Groupal changes all of this by introducing GROUP DYNAMICS into the
  purchase decision:

  1. SHOPPING BECOMES SOCIAL WITH REAL FINANCIAL CONSEQUENCES
     When inviting a friend to a group buy literally reduces your own price,
     shopping becomes a collaborative, community-driven activity. People
     stop scrolling alone and start buying together.

  2. PRICE IS NO LONGER FIXED — IT'S EARNED
     The Groupal model introduces the concept of "earned price" — the
     final price reflects how engaged and motivated the buyer community
     was. The most successful deals reward the most active participants.

  3. SELLERS AND BUYERS BECOME ALIGNED
     In traditional retail, sellers want prices high and buyers want them
     low — an adversarial relationship. On Groupal, both sides benefit
     from a large, engaged group: the seller moves volume, the buyer
     gets a massive discount. Their interests become aligned.

  4. WORD OF MOUTH IS MONETIZED FOR BUYERS, NOT JUST BRANDS
     Affiliate marketing and influencer culture pay brands for buyer
     recommendations. Groupal flips this: the BUYERS get paid (in
     savings) for spreading the word. The value flows to the community.

  5. A NEW DEFINITION OF SMART SHOPPING
     Groupal redefines what it means to be a smart consumer. It's not
     just about finding the lowest listed price — it's about building
     a buying group, timing your join strategically, and leveraging
     your network to drive the price even lower.

  6. ACCESSIBLE LUXURY AND BIG-TICKET ITEMS FOR EVERYONE
     Products that are aspirational for most people become attainable
     when the price is slashed by 30–50% through collective action.
     Groupal democratizes access to quality goods.
